ABOUT CAM
Cam is a television news and sports producer graduating with a broadcast journalism degree from the University of Maryland. He has experience as a reporter and multimedia journalist and produces video, audio, and written content for various outlets.
He works as a producer for Capital News Service (CNS-TV), the national, award-winning student-powered newscast at the University of Maryland. He formerly reported for CNS-TV from Capitol Hill and across Maryland and worked as a news producing intern at WRGB-TV CBS 6 Albany.
Cam is an avid sports fan and an athlete - he served the President of the University of Maryland Ice Hockey team. His passion for media and sports combine in his work. He co-hosts and produces the Brews and Bruins Podcast, and has worked as a Boston Bruins writer for The Hockey Writers for more than four years. He formerly worked as a video and production intern for Mid-Atlantic Sports Network (MASN), the television home of the Baltimore Orioles and the Washington Nationals, and as a sports production assistant for the Big Ten Network's Student U.
Cam has experience in radio as well, working as a four-year broadcaster and radio host for WMUC Sports. He also worked promotions for iHeart Media in Albany, NY.
